Don't feel like waiting for an online delivery? Enjoy the convenience and speed of Find In-Store, available across all United States stores.

It’s really that easy. Just order online and pick up your Honey order from your selected store, usually within three hours*! Your order may be sent from our warehouse to the store, if this is the case it will take 2-5 days to arrive.

If you have opted to pick up your order instore you will receive an email to confirm that your order is ready to collect. Please wait for this email before heading in to collect your goodies.

When can I collect my Find In-Store order?
After placing your order, you will receive a 'Ready For Collection' email, usually within three hours. Once you get the notification, simply visit your selected store to pick up your order.

How long do you hold Find In-Store orders?
We will hold your order for 30 days from the date it is ready for collection. Unfortunately, we are unable to extend this timeframe, so be sure to collect your order in time!

What if I can't make it to collect my order within 30 days?
If your order is not collected within 30 days of the collection date, it will no longer be available for collection. You will receive an email from us confirming that we have cancelled the order and provide you with a refund. Refunds will be processed in line with our Returns and Exchanges Policy.

What do I need to bring to collect my Find In-Store order?
To pick up your order, just be ready to show a valid photo ID (passport, driver’s license), your credit card if you used it to pay for this order and your ready for collection email.

Are there any Find In-Store fees?
No. There is no delivery fee or minimum spend at checkout required for a Find In-Store order.

  • Honey is not responsible for applicable customs fees, import duties, taxes, or any other charges for international orders.
  • For international orders, you will be responsible for all duties, taxes and customs charges which will be calculated at the time of your order and collected at checkout.
  • Duties, taxes and customs charges vary from country to country. In the European Union, duties and taxes can be 20% or more of the total declared value of your order. We recommend contacting your local customs office for further information.
  • Some countries may require additional government charges, they must be paid by you in order for your package to clear customs. 
  • Any additional international fees not paid at checkout will need to be paid by the customer to the local carrier within your country.
  • Please note if you refuse any shipments upon delivery, you will still be responsible for duties, taxes and customs fees incurred in shipping the package.
  • Duties & Taxes are non-refundable once your order has shipped. Any items returned will not be eligible for a refund on the duties & taxes associated.
  • NOTE: Honey does not make any profit on our shipping services to international customers and has made every effort to minimize our international shipping charges. Honey is legally required to disclose the full value of the package contents.
