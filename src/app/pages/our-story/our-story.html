About Honey

Honey is created by women and desired by all - a brand born to inspire confidence, empowerment, and unapologetic pleasure. At the forefront of luxury lingerie, our designs are bold, playful, and a little bit naughty…

Founded in 2006 with our first boutique in Australia, Honey has evolved into a global powerhouse, with a daring online presence and boutiques around the world. Every piece we create is crafted to celebrate sensuality, spark confidence, and make you feel incredible from the inside out.
